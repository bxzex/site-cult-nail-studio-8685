Knowledge

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Russian Manicure?

A Russian manicure is a dry technique that uses an electronic file (e-file) with specialized diamond bits to gently and precisely remove the cuticle and dead skin around the nail bed. This allows polish to be applied much closer to the root, resulting in a cleaner look that lasts significantly longer before regrowth shows.

How long does Hard Gel last?

With proper care, our hard gel enhancements typically last 3 to 4 weeks without chipping or lifting. Hard gel is non-porous and incredibly durable, making it an excellent choice for long-term wear and natural nail growth.

Do you use acrylics?

No, we are a strictly hard gel and soft gel studio. We believe gel products offer a superior, more flexible, and healthier alternative to traditional acrylics, without the strong odors or potential for severe nail damage.

What is a Smart Pedicure?

A Smart Pedicure is an innovative, waterless pedicure method using an e-file with disposable abrasive discs. It safely and effectively removes calluses and dry skin without the use of harsh chemicals or blades, leaving your feet incredibly smooth for weeks.
